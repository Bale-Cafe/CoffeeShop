About Us :
Our love of coffee inspired the concept for Bale Cafe. Actually, our original plan was to construct it as our HOUSE, but we decided to convert it into a COFFEE SHOP instead. We expect that Bale Cafe would develop and expand in the future, allowing us to construct a second floor.
Bale Café would also want to express its gratitude to DTI Pampanga for giving us the chance to contribute some of our business insights to your "Amánu" E-Coffee Table book. Being a part of it is an honor and a privilege.
